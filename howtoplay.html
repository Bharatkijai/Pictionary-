HOW TO PLAY

1.Divide into Teams

a.Split players into two or more teams.

b.Each team needs at least two players (one as the Picturist and others as guessers).

 

2.Set Up the Game

a.Arrange the game board (if used), word cards, drawing supplies, and a timer.

b.Place team tokens on the starting space of the board.

 

3.Decide Starting Team

a.Select which team will start (e.g., by rolling a die or random choice).

b.The first Picturist is chosen from this team.

 

4.Picturist Draws a Card

a.The Picturist for the team picks a word or phrase card from the deck.

b.They do not show it to anyone and begin planning their drawing.

 

5.Start the Timer

a.Set the timer for 60 seconds and let the drawing begin!

 

6.The Picturist Draws

a.The Picturist must illustrate the word corresponding to the color of the space where the token is located, using only pictures.

b.No letters, numbers, or verbal clues are allowed.

c.Arrows and symbols are allowed as part of the drawing.

 

7.Team Guesses

a.The Picturist’s team guesses the word or phrase based on the drawing.

b.Unlimited guesses are allowed within the time limit.

 

8.Scoring

a.If the team guesses correctly, they score a point or move forward on the board.

b.If they don’t guess correctly, no points are scored.

 

9.Rotate Picturists

a.After each turn, the next team chooses a new Picturist, and the process repeats.

 

10.Winning

a.Continue playing until one team, reaches the end of the board (if using one).

b.Achieves a pre-decided number of points.
